# 01-platform-business.md

# Platform Business Overview

Version: 1.0

## Purpose

This document explains the business vision and operating model of the AI
Sales Employee Platform.

It is intended for architects, engineers, AI agents, product owners and
business stakeholders.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Vision

Build an AI-native SaaS platform that behaves like a highly skilled
digital sales employee.

Instead of simply storing customer information, the platform actively:

-   Captures leads
-   Qualifies prospects
-   Recommends products
-   Schedules appointments
-   Automates follow-ups
-   Assists sales teams
-   Measures business outcomes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Objectives

-   Increase lead conversion
-   Reduce manual effort
-   Improve response time
-   Deliver consistent customer engagement
-   Scale sales operations without proportional headcount

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Business Capabilities

-   Lead Management
-   Customer Management
-   Conversation Management
-   Appointment Management
-   Workflow Automation
-   AI Assistance
-   Notification Management
-   Billing
-   Analytics
-   Marketplace

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Target Industries

Platform Core remains industry independent.

Industry plugins provide specialized business behavior for:

-   Real Estate
-   Automobile
-   Education
-   Healthcare
-   Insurance

------------------------------------------------------------------------

combining AI, workflows, events, and business automation.
