1. Executive Summary

Vision: Build an AI Sales Employee Platform that automatically converts ad leads into qualified appointments across Real Estate, Automobile, Education, and Healthcare using agentic AI, WhatsApp, and Voice AI.
Core Value Proposition: “We are building an AI employee that automatically turns your ad leads into appointments and sales, without your sales team doing any work.”
Proactive Engagement

AI calls/messages leads within 5 seconds

Omnichannel

Seamless switching between WhatsApp, Voice, SMS, Email

Agentic AI

Autonomous agents with Workflow Engine

Enterprise-Grade

Multi-tenant, secure, scalable

Notification Request │ ▼ Channel Routing ├── WhatsApp → Success → Done ├── WhatsApp → Failed → SMS → Success → Done ├── WhatsApp → Failed → SMS → Failed → Email → Success → Done └── All Failed → Log to Dead Letter → Human Alert

18. AI Cost Protection

✅ Critical Addition: Prevent unexpected LLM costs with tenant budgets.
Tenant Budget = ₹5000/month │ ▼ 80% → Warning Notification │ ▼ 100% → Throttle AI (slower/cheaper models) │ ▼ 120% → Suspend AI (human fallback only)
ThresholdAction
80%Warning notification to tenant
100%Throttle to cheaper model + alert
120%Suspend AI, route all leads to human
